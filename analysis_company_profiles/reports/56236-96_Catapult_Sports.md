## 8. Additional Company Information

**Description**: Catapult Group International Ltd is a sports data and analytics company. Its principal activities include the sale of wearables subscriptions, wearable units, and hardware, the rendering of software services, and content licensing; all related to sports. The company's three main operating segments are Performance & Health, Tactics & Coaching, Media & Other. Maximum revenue is derived from the Performance & Health segment, which involves designing, developing, and supplying wearable technology and athlete monitoring software solutions to sports teams, athletes, & the prosumer market. Geographically, it derives key revenue from the EMEA ( Europe, Middle East, and Africa) region, and the rest from the USA, Asia Pacific, Australia, and other areas.
