## 8. Additional Company Information

**Description**: Developer of a brain stimulation headset designed to target both the physical and behavioral elements of major depressive disorder. The company's headset combines brain stimulation with behavioral therapy in a fully automated mobile application based on peer-reviewed scientific literature, enabling individuals to restore activity in brain cells resulting in a reduction of depressive symptoms.
