## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This analysis examines the relationship between funding amounts, valuations, and exit outcomes for 14 wearable technology companies, with 4 successful IPO exits (28.6% exit rate). The data reveals **strong positive correlation (0.899)** between total funding raised and exit valuation, along with exceptional capital efficiency in successful exits.

### KEY METRICS
- **Exit Rate**: 28.6% (4 of 14 companies)
- **Funding-Exit Correlation**: 0.899 (strong positive correlation)
- **Average Exit Multiple**: 29.6x (excluding infinite outlier)
- **Exited Companies Avg Funding**: $2,392.92M
- **Private Companies Avg Funding**: $171.86M
- **Funding Premium for Exits**: 1,292% more funding

### Implications:
- **Patient Capital Required**: Investors should expect 10+ year holding periods

- **Capital Intensity**: Wearable tech is capital-intensive (hardware + software + scale)
